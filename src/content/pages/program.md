---
title: Program
description: Tentative workshop format and schedule for TPM 2026.
---

 We plan the workshop in a hybrid format. Presentations and talks will be delivered in person, streamed via Zoom, and recorded when speakers agree. Each accepted paper will present a spotlight talk that is pre-recorded and hosted on YouTube. We will then hold a poster session, possibly split in two depending on room space. Invited talks may also be pre-recorded if necessary and will still be live-streamed.
